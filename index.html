The Way of Fist & Foot

"Tae" (Foot), "Kwon" (Fist), and "Do" (Way). A systematic Korean martial art aimed at peace and self-control.

History & Origins

Officially declared in 1955, but tracing back 2,000 years to the Koguryo Dynasty. Founded on the principles of the Hwarang warriors: loyalty, obedience, honor, courage, and justice.
